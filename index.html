3D Controls

Desktop (Mouse)

  • Rotate: Left-click + drag
  • Zoom: Scroll wheel
  • Pan: Right-click + drag

Desktop (Trackpad)

  • Rotate: Click + drag
  • Zoom: Pinch gesture or two-finger scroll
  • Pan: Two-finger click + drag

Mobile (Touch)

  • Rotate: One finger drag
  • Zoom: Pinch with two fingers
  • Pan: Two finger drag
